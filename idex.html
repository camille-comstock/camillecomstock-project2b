About Me

I was born and raised in Marblehead, Massachusetts which is about 15 miles north of Boston.

I started taking vocal lessons in third grade and immediately, I fell in love with music.

I played piano in elementary school and middle school and guitar in high school.

I am currently a sophomore, majoring in Music Industry Studies at Loyola University New Orleans.

I love learning about the Music Business and collaborating with artists of all genres of music.
